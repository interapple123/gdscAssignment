Description

Cauliflower is all the rage now and it’s about time you gave it a try in these spicy tacos. I promise once you try it, there’s no turning back and you won’t miss the meat at all in these delicious tacos! The tangy and creamy spice along with the spicy roasted cauliflower and fresh and crispy toppings is a match made and heaven and will make your taste buds dance and beg for more!

Ingredients

For the Cauliflower

  • 1 head cauliflower chopped into bite-sized pieces
  • 3 Tbsp avocado oil or oil of choice
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder or taco seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp kosher salt

Creamy Cilantro Sauce

  • ¼ cup greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup sour cream or mayo
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro
  • 1 clove garlic
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper

To Assemble

  • 8 small Tortillas flour or corn
  • 1 Avocado sliced or diced
  • 1 cup Red cabbage shredded
  • 1 Jalapeno sliced
  • 1/4 cup Cilantro minced

Instructions

To Make Roasted Cauliflower

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a large rimmed baking sheet with foil or parchment paper.

  2. In a large bowl, combine the cauliflower florets, spiced, and oil. Stir until the cauliflower is coated with spices.

  3. Transfer to a baking sheet and bake 20 minutes or until lightly charred and cooked through.

To Make The Cilantro Sauce

  1. Mince the cilantro and garlic and whisk in a medium bowl or Add all the ingredients to the food processor, season with salt and pepper. Blend until smooth and creamy. Taste and adjust salt and pepper to taste.

To Assemble

  1. Warm tortillas over an open flame on the stovetop (does not work or electric). Place about 1/4 cup of cauliflower on a tortilla. Top with avocado, cabbage, sliced jalapeno, minced cilantro, and a generous drizzle of cilantro sauce. Serve immediately!
